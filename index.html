Feelings Jeopardy

How to Play

Make teams and have the players take turns selecting cards (Color and Point).

Click on a card to reveal the feeling or point value.

Use "Show All Cards" to flip all cards at once without zooming.

Change difficulty or randomize cards using the controls above.
